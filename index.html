Services

Residential Cleaning (Biweekly, Monthly, Weekly & One Time Cleaning)

If cleaning your home has become too much and you’re in need of professional residential cleaning, Orlandi's Cleaning is simply the best, most affordable choice in Columbus. When you book a house cleaning with Orlandi's Cleaning you gain access to a team of pre-vetted, professional cleaners who are committed to making your home sparkle and bringing you peace of mind.

After Renovation Cleaning

After renovation cleaning is a big and demanding job. It requires a lot of knowledge, patience, time and elbow grease. Not handling these cleaning requirements properly can cause the property owner damage, loss of money and time. You can count on Orlandis cleaning for this type of cleaning.

Commercial Cleaning

Do you want your office or business establishment to be cleaned by experts to get the best results? Orlandi's Cleaning is the right company for you then as we offer professional commercial cleaning services in Columbus. Here, we aim to make your office or shop fully clean to help retain their appeal. Moreover, we clean with patience and dedication to achieve satisfactory results. A commercial area needs to look appealing at all times as it helps you to attract your clients and customers. And to maintain the cleanliness, however, you will need to hire a company that provides the best commercial cleaning service in Columbus.

Move-in / Move-out Cleaning

Did you know that moving is one of the most stressful events in a person’s life? If you’ve ever moved, or are in the midst of one, you may not find this fact all too surprising. It’s so much more than just transporting your belongings from one location to another. There are countless tasks and processes you must endure before you can comfortably settle into your new dwelling. Cleaning doesn’t need to be one of them. Orlandi's Cleaning can provide move in and move out cleaning services to take a much needed load off your plate.

Post Construction

Have you recently finished a home/office improvement project and find yourself left with a mess? The construction can be stressful enough without having to deal with the post-job mess it leaves behind. Orlandi's Cleaning post construction cleaning services will help your place make the transition from construction site to homestead.
